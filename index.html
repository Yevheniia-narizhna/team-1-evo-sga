04Game Description

Criminal Archives 2: Extra is an immersive detective puzzle game where players dive into the world of crime investigations. Explore detailed crime scenes, solve complex puzzles, and uncover hidden clues to solve thrilling mysteries. With challenging levels, deep storylines, and intriguing cases, the game brings a true detective experience. Every investigation is a unique journey with new challenges that will test your analytical skills and detective instincts.
