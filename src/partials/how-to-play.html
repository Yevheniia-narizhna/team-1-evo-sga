03 How to Play

  • Investigate Crime Scenes:

    Examine each crime scene carefully for clues and evidence.

  • Solve Puzzles:

    Tackle puzzles and challenges that will lead you closer to solving the mystery.

  • Analyze Evidence:

    Use your analytical skills to connect the dots and uncover the truth behind each case.

  • Unlock New Cases:

    As you solve each case, unlock new mysteries to investigate.
